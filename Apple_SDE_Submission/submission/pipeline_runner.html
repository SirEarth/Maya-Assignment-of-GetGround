Why aren't these blocked? Low-confidence harmonise is a SEMANTIC-stage rule (severity MEDIUM, policy flag-and-keep). Three reasons we deliberately don't gate:
① The match might be a genuinely new SKU not yet in the Product Reference (a real product that should be added to the dictionary, not discarded). ② The harmonise score is a soft signal — its weighting can drift; gating on it would couple data retention to algorithm calibration. ③ Business reviewers in the Task C-2 loop need access to the row + its raw_payload JSONB to decide whether to add a dictionary entry, retire an old SKU, or mark IGNORED. Hard-blocking would silently drop data and hide that decision.
